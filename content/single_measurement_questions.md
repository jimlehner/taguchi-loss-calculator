# Expected Loss Questions

## QUESTION_1
Question: What is the expected loss due to poor quality when the measured value for the quality characteristic is 29 units?

Options:
- $3.00
- $0.00
- $0.56
- $1.50

Correct: $0.56

Success:
Correct! As the value of a quality or performance characteristic approaches the target, loss due to poor quality decreases.

## QUESTION_3
Question: What is the expected loss due to poor quality when the cost of scrap is $7.50, the value for the quality characteristic is 31.75 units, the USL is 36 units, and the LSL is 25 units?

Options:
- $10.00
- $0.39
- $7.50
- $0.56

Correct: $0.39

Success:
Correct! When the cost of scrap is $7.50, the measured quality characteristic is 31.75 units, the USL is 36, and the LSL is 25, the expected loss is $0.39. The wider specification limits increase the process "elbow room," decreasing the expected loss.
