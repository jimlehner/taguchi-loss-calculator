# About the author

**Jim Lehner** is a manufacturing and process engineering professional who had the good fortune of discovering the work of Walter A. Shewhart and Donald J. Wheeler early in his career. Putting the theories and methods of these two men to practice, Jim has reduced the costs and improved the quality of business and manufacturing processes across the automotive, defense, aerospace, industrial machining, and medical device industries. His success in these endeavors is the result of an approach that marries the power of modern data infrastructure and a hands-on mentality with a formal engineering education. Jim is a graduate of Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI), where he earned a Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering and Master of Science in Manufacturing Engineering.
